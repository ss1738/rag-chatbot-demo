## About Acme Robotics
Acme Robotics builds autonomous warehouse robots for mid-size logistics companies. Founded in
2019, headquartered in Bristol, UK, with a support team in Bengaluru, India.

## Products
- **Acme Mover M1**, a compact floor robot that carries up to 80 kg. Battery life is 9 hours
  of continuous operation; it recharges to 80% in 45 minutes.
- **Acme Mover M2**, carries up to 150 kg, battery life 7 hours, adds obstacle-avoidance LIDAR.
- **Acme Fleet OS**, the software that coordinates a fleet of robots, plans routes, and avoids
  collisions. Sold as an annual subscription.

## Pricing
- Acme Mover M1: £6,500 per unit.
- Acme Mover M2: £9,200 per unit.
- Acme Fleet OS: £4,000 per year for up to 10 robots; £300/year per additional robot.
- Volume discount: 12% off hardware for orders of 10 or more units.

## Warranty & Support
- All hardware includes a **2-year warranty** covering manufacturing defects.
- Support hours are 09:00-18:00 IST, Monday to Friday, via support@acme-robotics.example.
- Critical outages are handled 24/7 for Fleet OS subscribers on the Priority plan.
- Batteries are considered consumables and are warrantied for 12 months.

## Returns Policy
Hardware can be returned within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund if unused and in
original packaging. After 30 days, returns are handled case by case under warranty. Software
subscriptions are non-refundable but can be cancelled to stop the next renewal.

## Safety
Robots must operate within mapped zones. The M2's LIDAR stops the robot within 20 cm of an
unexpected obstacle. Firmware updates are mandatory and are pushed automatically overnight.

## Onboarding
A typical deployment takes 2-3 weeks: site survey (week 1), floor mapping and Fleet OS setup
(week 2), and staff training plus go-live (week 3). Acme provides an on-site engineer for the
go-live day.

- **How long does the M1 battery last?** 9 hours of continuous operation.

- **Can I return a robot?** Yes, within 30 days if unused and in original packaging.
- **Do you offer volume discounts?** Yes, 12% off hardware for 10+ units.
- **What are support hours?** 09:00-18:00 IST, Mon-Fri (24/7 for Priority Fleet OS users).
